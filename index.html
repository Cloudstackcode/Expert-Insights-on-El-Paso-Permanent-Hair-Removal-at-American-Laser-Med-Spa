What to Expect During Your Treatment

Understanding the process can help ease any concerns you might have about permanent hair removal. At American Laser Med Spa – El Paso, we prioritize your comfort and safety every step of the way.

Before the procedure, we prepare your skin and explain what you will experience during and after the treatment. You might feel a slight warming sensation or a gentle snap as the laser pulses, but most of our clients find the discomfort manageable.

Treatments usually take between 15 to 45 minutes depending on the size of the area being treated. Because hair grows in cycles, multiple sessions are necessary to target hair at different growth stages. On average, clients require between six to eight sessions spaced several weeks apart.

After the treatment, it’s normal to experience some redness or mild swelling, but these effects typically subside within a few hours. We provide aftercare guidance to support skin healing and to protect the treated area from irritation or sun exposure.
